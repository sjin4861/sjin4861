# Hi, i'm Jun Seo 👋
I am a Master's student at the **POSTECH NLP Lab** (Pohang University of Science and Technology), Republic of Korea, where I am privileged to be advised by **Prof. Gary Geunbae Lee** and **Prof. Hyounghun Kim**. My research lies at the intersection of **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **AI in Education (AIEd)**, with a primary focus on **Knowledge Tracing** and **Student Simulation**.

Before joining graduate school, I taught high school mathematics to students with diverse learning needs. That experience led me to a central research question: how can AI support genuinely personalized learning in real educational settings? My current work aims to bridge advanced language technologies and practical educational applications.

## 🌱 Research Interests
AI in Education, Knowledge Tracing, Student Simulation
